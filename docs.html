INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the Plano West NHS Documentation. This page hosts all relevant information for PWNHS members regarding the club and its operations.

What is PWNHS?

Plano West NHS is the most monumental service organisation in the greater DFW area. We are devoted to aiding the community and exemplifying what it means to be an honor student. You can check out our Four Pillars for more information on NHS foci.

Why Should I Join PWNHS?

The National Honor Society offers many benefits, both to your social and academic experience at West. Some of these benefits may be found here. It never hurts to say you were part of the nation's premier high school organisation!

GENERAL CONSIDERATIONS


Volunteer Hours

Members must complete 15 hours of volunteer service each semester to remain in good standing with the Plano West NHS. Be sure that your NHS hours come from approved organisations and that you abide by our restrictions.

Penalty Hours

They accumulate quickly! 2 hours for an abscense at a mandatory meeting, missed + 5 hours for every semester you don't complete 15 hours. Try not to get them, and complete them quickly if you do. Our penalty hours tab dives into more detail.

Obligations

Make sure to observe honorable behavior. Violation of the law, school conduct code, or academic integrity will result in immediate expulsion. Be sure to review your obligations.

VOLUNTEER HOURS

Volunteering is a huge part of NHS. Your ability to aid the community around you goes a long way to demonstrating your capabilities as an individual. We're sure that you will do many amazing things during your volunteer services this year.

Hours Sheet

You can download the PWNHS volunteer hours sheet next to this block.Download Hours Sheet (.docx)

Guidelines for Hours

Please keep in mind that when filling out your sheet, there should be no crossing out and rewriting of hours, names, or signatures - this is required to eliminate suspicion of fraud. If you must cross out a parameter, cross out the entire box and rewrite your hours in another one.

Multiple dates of the same service may be confined to one box, so long as each date is noted to the side and initialed.

All members must complete 15 hours volunteer hours each semester. No double dipping! Hours turned in for NHS do not count for another club, and vice versa; we do cross-check.

All fall hours must be turned in by Dec. 4.

Restrictions

Up to 10 hours from the summer may count towards NHS, and may only be redeemed during the fall semester.

Up to 10 hours from one organisation may count towards NHS. The rest must be from other sources.

Don't wear yourself out! You may only volunteer 8 hours at a time (at one organisation on one day).

Only 5 hours may be from PTSA/Booster Club events. In addition, these events must be approved by the PWNHS. Approval is done on a case-by-case, rolling basis, so contact us as soon as possible if you think you may want to use one of these events for your hours!

The organisations you volunteer at must be sanctioned as "approved" by either the national branch of the NHS or the local PWNHS. Don't know if your organisation is approved?

As unfortunate as it may be, only secular participation from approved organisations is accepted for valid NHS hours. In other words,no religiously-afilliated volunteering may be used for your NHS volunteer hours. This includes, but is not limited to, church/mosque/temple events, religious charity, and the like.

Donation/Fundraising events, helping teachers, and Eagle/Boy/Girl Scouts WILL NOT COUNT FOR NHS HOURS.

Relationships

The Plano West NHS encourages you to build a lasting, concerted relationship with one or more of these organizations and to give back to your community on a regular basis. It looks great and makes you feel even better!

PENALTY HOURS

Sometimes you can't make two meetings or aren't able to finish all your hours. That's okay! But to be fair to all of our members and adhere to NHS guidelines, we must give penalty hours to all those who are not able to fulfill PWNHS guidelines.

What Incites Penalty Hours?

Failure to complete the 15 required volunteer hours each semester will result in penalty hours for the next semester. The number of penalty hours will be defined as the number of uncompleted horus + 5. For instance, if you only finish 12 hours one semester, you will have missed 3 and have to make up 3 + 5 = 8 hours the next semester in addition to the normal, required 15 hours per semester (i.e. you will have to do 15 + 8 = 23 hours in total for the next semester).

You are required to attend two of four PWNHS meetings each semester. Failure to miss one required meeting will result in 2 penalty hours. Failure to miss both required meetings will result in 4 penalty hours. Penalty hours for missed meetings must be turned in the same semester the meetings were unattended.

Completing Penalty Hours

You can download the penalty hours sheet next to this block. Download Penalty Hours Sheet (.docx)

Turn in your penalty hours sheet(s) together with your regular hours sheets by the required due date(s). Your completion of penalty hours must abide by the same guidelines and restrictions as your regular volunteer hours.

MEETINGS

Meetings are our primary chance to interact with the PWNHS community and provide you with updates on the club and our evolving progress. They may be big, but they hold a lot of essential information.

General

Meetings are held on specified dates during A/B Lunch or during 7th Period (for our zero-hour friends).

Of the four meetings each semester, you are required to attend two. During the fall, you must attend the first meeting (informational meeting) and one of the other three meetings (growth meeting).

Miss a Meeting?

If you miss a meeting, don't fret! Attend another one so that you can get your two required meetings.

Try not to miss our informational meeting, as it is the keynote meeting of the year. If you are unfortuantely unable to make it, download and go through the informational meeting powerpoint (found below) and make up the meeting by attending both growth meetings. Or, you can just do two penalty hours in addition to the required 15.
